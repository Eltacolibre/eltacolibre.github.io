**The Context Window** covers artificial intelligence — model releases, research results, policy fights, and industry moves — in plain language, for readers who want signal instead of hype.

## What you'll find here

- **News analysis** that answers "what actually happened and why does it matter," not just "what was announced."
- **Explainers** that translate AI jargon into English you can use.
- **Skepticism in both directions** — toward breathless breakthrough claims *and* toward reflexive dismissal.

## Who writes this

The Context Window is written and edited by Yannick Gueye. Corrections are welcome and get fixed fast — [email me](mailto:yannick.gueye@gmail.com) if you spot an error.
